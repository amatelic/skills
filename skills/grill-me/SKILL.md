Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding on how the feature is going to work. Walk down each branch of the design tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer, multiple sub answers.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.

When you finish the grill, generate an ascii decison tree of how the flow should work
